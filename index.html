Heiland JS Drawer

About Heiland JS Drawer

Heiland JS Drawer is a user-friendly web application that allows users to effortlessly create geometric shapes and constructions within the browser environment. With its intuitive interface, users can select three arbitrary points within the client area of the browser, and the application dynamically generates visual representations based on these points.


Upon selecting three points, the application highlights their locations by drawing red circles with a diameter of 11 pixels, centered on each selected point. This feature provides users with a clear visual reference for the points they've chosen.


In addition to highlighting the selected points, Heiland JS Drawer utilizes advanced geometric algorithms to create two additional shapes based on the user's input:


  • Blue Parallelogram: This shape is constructed using the three points selected by the user. The application calculates the necessary parameters to draw a parallelogram, ensuring that it accurately represents the geometric properties defined by the user's input. The parallelogram is drawn in blue, providing a distinct visual contrast to the red circles representing the selected points.

  • Yellow Circle: Based on the parallelogram created using the three selected points, Heiland JS Drawer calculates a circle with the same area and center of mass as the parallelogram. This circle is drawn in yellow, providing users with a comprehensive visual representation of the geometric relationships between the selected points and the constructed shapes.

Frequently Asked Question - How it works ?

How to draw ?

Drawing shapes never been so easy, it only requires three steps :

1. First , you should refer to the white board as identified in the picture below with a red rectangle.

2. Secondly, In the red rectangle area, with your mouse, select 3 seperate position and click on each accordingly. Each time you click a red dot appears.

3. Last and after the third click, the parallelogram and the circle will be drawn automatically.

In the following video, you can see how to click on the white board to draw the shapes.

How to reshape ?

To reshape the existing shapes :

1. First, you should refer to one of the red dots.

2. Secondly, with your mouse, click on a red dot and drag it to a new position. The parallelogram and the circle will be reshaped accordingly.

In the following video, you can see how to reshape the shapes by dragging the red dots.

How to reset and delete shapes ?

To reset and delete the existing shapes :

1. First and lastly, you only have to click on the blue button Reset on the top right of your screen. Once you click on it, the shapes will disappear from the screen.

In the following video, you can see how to reset the shapes by clicking on the blue reset button.
